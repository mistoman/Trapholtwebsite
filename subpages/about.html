    About the museum

    Trapholt is a museum of contemporary art and design located in
    Kolding, Denmark.

    It opened in 1988 and was previously named Trapholt Kunstmuseum
    (Trapholt Museum of Art) but its increasing focus on the broader arts
    lead to its shortened name.
