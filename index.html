    the trapholt story

    Trapholt is a museum of contemporary art and design located in Kolding, Denmark.
    It opened in 1988 and was previously named Trapholt Kunstmuseum
    (Trapholt Museum of Art) but its increasing focus on the broader arts lead
    to its shortened name. It describes itself as a “museum for modern
    painting, crafts, design, and furniture design”.
